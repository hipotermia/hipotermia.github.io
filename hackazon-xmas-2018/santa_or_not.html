[200] Santa or no Santa?

Are you seeing Santa Claus or not?

On this challenge a picture santa_or_not.png is given.

Looking at it, it's possible to determine the file is a jpg instead of png.

root@kali:~/hackazon/santa_or_not# file santa_or_not.png 
santa_or_not.png: JPEG image data, JFIF standard 1.01, aspect ratio, density 1x1, segment length 16, baseline, precision 8, 17400x17400, frames 3
root@kali:~/hackazon/santa_or_not# mv santa_or_not.png santa_or_not.jpg

Here is the resulting picture.

Zoom in the picture to be able to see smaller pictures of Santa Claus and Christmas trees.

Now comes the tedious job, paint every picture of Santa Claus in black to obtain a QR code.

Scan the QR with any scanner to obtain the flag.
